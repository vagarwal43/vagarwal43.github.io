BENEFITS OF TAEKWONDO

It is no secret that learning martial arts can benefit a person in many ways.Taekwondo being an olympic sports not only helps us in staying fit and healthy but also helps us in mental health well-being.
The exercise element includes flexibility, balance, strength and posture.
The mental element includes increased confidence, increased self-esteem,teaches you social skills and self-discipline. There are many unseen benefits too.
Taekwondo teaches us self-defence which helps us when any mishappening happens with us. This is one sport that teaches us how to protect ourselves in danger.

Lets dig deeper into the benefits of Taekwondo.

Health Benefits

The benefits of taekwondo in healthare as follows:

  • Balance and stability
  • Flexibility
  • Strength and power
  • Stamina and endurance
  • Muscle Toning
  • Agility and Coordination
  • Speed and quirkiness

Social Skills Benefits

The benefits of taekwondo in social skills are as follows:

  • Meeting New Friends
  • Increases self-confidence
  • Builds Support System
  • Increases Leadership Qualities
  • Social outings

Mental Health Benefits

The benefits of taekwondo in mental health are as follows:

  • Stress Relief
  • Self control
  • Respect for self and others
  • Memrization skills
  • Breathing skills and meditation
  • Positive attitude
  • Focus and concentration

Unseen events Benefits

  • Limit kidapping
